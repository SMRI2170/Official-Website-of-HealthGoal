Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2024

Welcome to HealthGoal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App includes:

Personal Data You Provide

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when you register and use the App. This includes:

  • Goal Settings: Target weight, target completion date, and height.
  • Health Records: Daily weight records that you manually input.

Data from Health Services (with your permission)

When you grant us permission, we may collect data from third-party health services to provide a comprehensive view of your health.

  • Google Fit (for Android users): We may access and import your weight, step count, and sleep duration data.
  • Apple HealthKit (for iOS users): We may access and import your weight, step count, and sleep duration data.

This data is used solely within the App to display your progress and provide insights. We only read this data; we do not write or share it without your explicit consent.

How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your health data, including progress charts and summary cards.
  • Calculate and display your progress towards your goals (e.g., progress to target weight, required pace).
  • Provide personalized feedback and motivational messages.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your Personal Information with third parties for their marketing purposes. All data collected is for the sole purpose of the App's functionality. Your data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. Since all data is stored on your device, the security of your data is linked to the security of your device itself. We recommend using a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID to protect your device.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data within the App.

  • Access and Correction: You can view and update your goal settings and manually entered weight records at any time within the App.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete individual weight records by swiping on them in the history list. Uninstalling the App will remove all its data from your device.
  • HealthKit/Google Fit Permissions: You can manage the App's access to your health data at any time through the settings on your iOS or Android device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
